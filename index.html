Precis Writing Tips
  • Precis original passage ka 1/3 length jitna hona chahiye.
  • Apne words mein likho, copy-paste mat karo.
  • Sirf main idea aur key points rakho, examples/details hatao.
  • Title zaroor do precis ke upar.
  • Third person, past/reported tense use karo.
  • Ek hi paragraph mein likho, points list mat banao.
